### Changed
- Migrate app to use Dynamic Javascript.
  [9](https://github.com/OSC/bc_osc_stata/pull/9)

## [0.3.0] - 2020-01-20
### Changed
- in [5](https://github.com/OSC/bc_osc_stata/pull/5):

## [0.2.0] - 2020-11-18
### Added
- Added slurm-owens cluster to begin migrating Owens to Slurm in
